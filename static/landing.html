Questions

Asked by café owners, answered plainly

Do I need to change anything about my POS?
No. Mesa reads from Vendus through its official API. You keep ringing up sales exactly as you do today — Mesa works silently alongside.
Can Mesa break or modify anything in my Vendus account?
Impossible by design. The connection is read-only: Mesa can look at your sales and catalog, but cannot create, edit or delete anything. Your key is encrypted and never visible to our team.
I'm not technical. How hard is the setup, honestly?
Three minutes: paste one key (we show you exactly where it is in Vendus, with screenshots) and your dashboard fills itself with your real data. If Vendus is handled by your accountant, one click sends them the guide instead.
Where do the ingredient costs come from?
You enter them once — starting with just your top 10 sellers (about 5 minutes). Mesa's recipe engine does the rest: unit conversions, sub-recipes, and automatic margin updates whenever a supplier price changes.
Who can see my numbers?
You decide. Three roles: owner (everything), staff (recipes and stock only — no financials), investor (read-only). Your data is EU-hosted, GDPR-compliant, and exportable anytime.
